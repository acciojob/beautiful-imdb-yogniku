The film stars Ranbir Kapoor, Anil Kapoor, Bobby Deol, Rashmika Mandanna and Triptii Dimri. In the film, Ranvijay Singh, a violent young man, learns about an assassination attempt on his father and sets out to exact revenge. The film was officially announced in January 2021, along with the title of the film.
